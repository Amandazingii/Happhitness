Instructions For Users

1. Click on "Workout Challenge Levels" on homepage to access workout videos. A piano sound will play everytime the exercise changes, so listen out for it!



2. Recommended to start from Level One for beginners. However, feel free to start fast at Level Three if you want!



2. Do NOT attempt more than 2 sessions everyday. Rest and recovery is important!



3. To gain entry into Hall of Fame, athlete has to complete at least ONE full level. Click on Hall of Fame to find out more.
